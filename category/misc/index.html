Ghost Rider

This playset is the handiwork of Playtech Studios. It features five reels and 25 paylines. Ghost Rider also has a jackpot feature that provides a bountiful amount if won. In addition to this, the game is linked to two progressive slots; therefore, providing an incremental jackpot option for players who want to go all out. The graphics and music do not disappoint. Playtech ensures that whether you are playing the bonus round, or you are sweating trying to make a winning combination in the base game, you will enjoy the encounter thoroughly.

There are also other significant gaming features that are useful in making the payout even more. These are:

  • A wild symbol

Sadly, the game does not have a multiplier that would have been useful in making cash even more. Free spins are also not provided in this set. However, the game avails a pretty decent jackpot amount of 10,000 coins.

Spider-Man

This film has been in the entertainment scene for the longest time ever, and Cryptologic did not hesitate to relive this encounter on the reels. As much the slot is based on the movie, the creators were aiming at making it feel like a comic book. This aspect is depicted in the way the artwork is done. The central figures used are those of Spider-Man, trains, spiders, and Dr. Octopus.

Spider-Man Slot Machine has a multiplier and a free spins feature. These are used to increase your winnings significantly. However, unlike most slot sets that give you both options effortlessly, you have to choose between the two options in this game. This game also has a bonus round. To get to it, you have to acquire three or more scatter symbols. Once the game gets to this bonus mode, you will compete against Dr. Octopus for the gifts. You can win instant cash by playing the jackpot. To be eligible for this section, you have to play max bet; thus engaging all the 25 pay lines.

You will instantly get an additional free spin. Another thing that is notable about this game is the multiplier. Unfortunately, this feature is only active during the bonus spins turn.
